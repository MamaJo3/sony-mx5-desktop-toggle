XM5 Ambient — quick start
=========================

1. Double-click  Setup.cmd
   (If Windows SmartScreen warns "Windows protected your PC":
    click "More info" -> "Run anyway". It's unsigned, like most
    small open-source tools.)

2. Done! Press  Ctrl + Alt + A  to switch your Sony WH-1000XM5
   between Ambient Sound and Noise Cancelling.

3. To change the shortcut or behaviour (toggle vs hold, which modes,
   ambient level), open "XM5 Ambient Settings" - it's added to your
   Desktop and Start menu.

Requirements
------------
- Windows 10 (build 19041+) or Windows 11
- A Sony WH-1000XM5 paired AND connected to this PC over Bluetooth
- Nothing else - the .NET runtime is bundled in this download.

Uninstall
---------
Double-click  Uninstall.cmd
